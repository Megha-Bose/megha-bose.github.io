Jk. Hey there! I’m a software engineer on the Google Cloud Backup and Disaster Recovery team. My work revolves around designing and building systems that keep data safe, resilient, and recoverable at cloud scale across distributed environments.

I completed my B.Tech. and M.S. by Research in Computer Science from the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad . My research background lies at the intersection of machine learning, game theory, and cybersecurity.

When I’m not tinkering with stuff on my PC, I enjoy exploring visual and performing arts, getting lost in books, and writing. I mostly write as a way of wrapping up thoughts about anything and everything - things under the sun, beyond it, or maybe what does not even exist (yet).
